## Generative AI Evolution

### Large Language Models (LLMs)

Generative AI models have become increasingly sophisticated and specialized:

- **Larger context windows**: Models can process and understand significantly more text input
- **Specialized models**: Domain-specific models for healthcare, finance, legal, and technical domains
- **Improved efficiency**: Smaller, more efficient models delivering comparable performance
- **Multimodal capabilities**: Models that seamlessly handle text, images, and other modalities

### Fine-tuning & Customization

Organizations are increasingly fine-tuning models for specific use cases:
- Custom knowledge integration
- Industry-specific terminology and practices
- Proprietary business logic embedding
- Improved performance on specialized tasks

### Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

RAG systems combine language models with external knowledge sources:
- Real-time information integration
- Reduced hallucination through factual grounding
- Scalable knowledge management
- Improved accuracy for specific domains
